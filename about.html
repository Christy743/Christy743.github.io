I found that I really like to work in the world of code. I started out with the free learn to code websites such as Codecademy. I liked it! In my pursuit of learning more about code and web development, I have taken three 'Blueprints' for Skillcrush. Then I found that there were scholarships available for Flatiron School. They only accept 10% of applicants. I prayed about it and decided to go for it! I got accepted and received a scholarship that pays half of my tuition. I feel very blessed with my decision and with Flatiron School.

I live in Southeastern Wyoming on the prairie. I have my house on a few acres of land. I don't have any cattle or horses, but I have three adorable mixed breed dogs and two lovable cats. I would love to find a remote job, so I can stay in Wyoming. I've moved quite a bit in my life and I really don't plan to move again. I am a very hard working and loyal person.

Learning to code has been challenging and fun. I've met many fellow code learners and teachers along the way. I am glad I made the choice to take this journey!
